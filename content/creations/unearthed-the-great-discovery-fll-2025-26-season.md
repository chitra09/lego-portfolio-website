# The Story of the Great Discovery
### A FIRST LEGO League Innovation Project

On Goat Hill, a grazing goat stumbled upon something sparkling in the dirt — sparking a journey into archaeology, robotics, and history. Our team brought this discovery to life through a skit, a coded robot, and a set of core values.

**The Shiny Discovery** — A farmer notices something shiny while tending his goats and calls in archaeologists to investigate, roping off the site to protect it until the experts arrive.

**The Radar Vehicle** — Our team built and coded a robotic vehicle equipped with radar to scan underground without digging, helping locate artifacts safely before excavation begins.

**The Ancient Kitchen** — Archaeologists uncover an ancient cooking site, and the team imagines how people once prepared meals with clay pots — comparing it to how we cook today.

**How Our Robot Thinks** — Using color-coded programming blocks, we coded our vehicle to start, drive to the dig site, and return — carefully measuring distance so it could move forward, backward, and turn with precision.

## Our Core Values

- **Teamwork** — Like building with bricks, we took turns as Builder and Coder, and when something didn't work, we said "Let's try again!"
- **Discovery** — We learned new things about archaeology and how ancient people lived.
- **Innovation** — We used our imagination to solve problems along the way.
- **Fun** — Above all, we learned that working together and respecting each other makes every discovery feel like finding treasure.
